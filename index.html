OUR JOURNEY

FOUNDERS’ ORIGIN March 28, 2026
Built by Tanish + Aditya — Sagar to Katni

CodeVogue is the result of a long-running obsession with code and design. I first fell in love with programming in fifth grade, and two years ago I met Aditya at a hackathon. He comes from Katni, I come from Sagar — both from MP — and we discovered a shared taste for clean interfaces and fast, modern execution. Our spark became momentum when a team from Sagar took second place in the national KVS Vidyasetu AI Hackathon first edition hosted by IIT Delhi, beating nearly 950 KV schools. That moment supercharged our mission, and on 28th March 2026 we launched CodeVogue as a lean studio for premium, high-performance web experiences.
