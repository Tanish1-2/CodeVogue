1. Overview

HunterOS by CodeVogue respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we store it, and what choices you have when using HunterOS.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including your display name, email address, account preferences, selected timezone, theme settings, exported records, and profile data associated with your HunterOS account.

We may also process technical information needed to operate the platform, such as session details, browser-based preferences, and account state required for authentication, recovery, exports, or profile management.

3. How We Use Information

We use your information to operate HunterOS, authenticate your account, display your profile, save preferences, support exports such as CSV and PDF records, improve user experience, and maintain system security.

  • Run and personalize your HunterOS account.
  • Store system preferences like theme and timezone.
  • Enable recovery, export, and profile actions.
  • Detect misuse, abuse, or platform risks.

4. Local Storage and Device Data

HunterOS may use browser storage or similar local device storage to remember your selected theme, session preferences, and account-related settings for a smoother experience. Some settings may persist on your device until cleared by you or overwritten by the platform.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information only when required for service functionality, lawful compliance, security enforcement, fraud prevention, technical operations, or to protect HunterOS, CodeVogue, or other users.

6. Data Retention

We retain account and preference data for as long as necessary to provide the service, maintain records, resolve disputes, enforce platform rules, and comply with legal obligations, unless deletion is requested or required.

7. Exports and User Control

You may be able to export your HunterOS data in formats such as CSV or PDF. You may also request or trigger deletion features available within the system, but some technical, backup, fraud-prevention, or legal records may be retained where necessary.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect account data, but no website, device, or online system can guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for keeping your login details and recovery information secure.

9. Children

HunterOS is not intended for unlawful use by children in violation of applicable law. If you believe personal information was submitted improperly, contact us for review and removal where appropriate.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of HunterOS after updates means you accept the revised policy to the extent permitted by law.

11. Contact

For privacy questions, legal requests, or data concerns, contact CodeVogue through your official project contact email or support channel.

This is a general website policy draft and should be reviewed for jurisdiction-specific compliance if you launch commercially, collect sensitive data, or expand your processing activities.
